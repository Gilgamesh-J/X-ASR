Vibe XASR 2.0

  • Real-time streaming input — text types into the caret live as you speak, instead of pasting all at once on release (falls back to clipboard when there's no text field).
  • Hotword domain packs — pick your field (coding, medical, legal, finance, and more) and specialized terms auto-correct to their standard spelling.
  • Reworked AI cleanup ("AI 整理") — local + cloud dual-path polishing for clearer, more natural text.
  • Streamlined interface — compact HUD, refreshed onboarding, and a new "Delete app & data" option.
  • Fixes — text now inserts in WeChat; no more double-insertion in terminals / remote desktop (tmux).
  • 实时流式输入 —— 边说边把文字逐字写入光标处,不再松手后整段粘贴(无输入框时自动落到剪贴板)。
  • 热词场景库 —— 选择你的行业(编程、医疗、法律、金融等),专业词自动纠正为标准写法。
  • AI 整理重做 —— 本地 + 云端双路润色,文本更清晰自然。
  • 界面精简 —— 紧凑 HUD、重做的引导流程,新增「彻底删除软件与配置」。
  • 修复 —— 微信里现在能正常插入;终端 / 远程桌面(tmux)不再重复插入两遍。

macOS is the primary development branch; the Windows build is compiled in sync and not yet fully tested (preview). Feedback welcome.
